About this site:

Like many developers, I have a wide range of interests that extend beyond code. From art, to literature to programming, sometimes it gets a little difficult to keep track everything I am playing around with.

That's why I decided to move to GitHub: I can organize and store all of my projects in one location, and with GitHub pages can assemble one entry point that higlights all of the different things I am working on.

While the site might change over time, the initial intent is to maintain a list of the projects I am working on, support an occassional blog post and provide an easier way for me to document what it is that I am doing and why. The fact that it is public is also a big plus.

About me:

I currently live in Minneapolis with my wife and our cat, Ray. I spend a lot of time reading, drawing, biking and playing with code.

I have a degree in Art from the University of Minnesota and I have spent over 15 years working with technology in a variety of industries. I am currently a consultant working in Health Insurance.
