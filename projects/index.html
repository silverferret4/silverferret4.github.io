Simple Log

A work in progress, Simple Log is meant to be an easy to use, web-based utility that allows you to track anything you want in a truly customizable manner. From reading lists to workouts, you can create a log that tracks exactly the attributes you care about, even setting motiviational challenges to help you reach your desired goals.

Built with HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, and a little javascript.
